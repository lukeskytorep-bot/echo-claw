# Target 0006

**Target coordinates:** 5936 1589  
**Date of the target:** 15 July 2023  

**Target:** Castle Party Festival at Bolków Castle, Poland. A gothic and alternative music festival held inside a medieval castle complex, where people gather to socialize, move through the grounds, and listen to live performances on a stage placed within the castle area.

---

## Description

The target is a **live outdoor music festival** set inside a **medieval stone castle** located on elevated ground above a town. The environment combines historical architecture, natural landscape, and human activity into one layered event space.

---

## Terrain and Spatial Layout

The location consists of several clearly defined layers:

- **Hill / elevation:**  
  The castle stands on a raised hill and dominates the surrounding landscape. The ground slopes down toward the town.

- **Castle structure:**  
  A fortified stone complex with thick walls, internal courtyard areas, and controlled entry points. The walls create both enclosure and elevated observation points.

- **Festival zone:**  
  The inner and adjacent parts of the castle serve as the main event area, including the stage and audience space.

- **Park and greenery around the castle:**  
  Trees, grass, and uneven terrain surround the structure, softening the hard stone environment.

- **Town beside the castle:**  
  A town lies directly below and around the castle, with streets, buildings, and public areas. It acts as an extension of the movement flow connected to the event.

- **Paths and roads:**  
  Cobblestone and paved routes connect the gate, courtyard, slopes, and town. These paths function as movement corridors for the crowd.

The castle acts as a central node, with people moving between the town and the elevated festival area.

---

## Castle Appearance

The castle has a rough, heavy, and weathered appearance. Its main features include:

- Massive stone walls with visible aging and irregular texture
- Medieval architecture that feels functional rather than decorative
- Enclosed and semi-open zones created by wall segments
- An entrance gate that acts as a threshold
- Elevated edges and walls used as seating or observation points

The structure feels strong, grounded, and dominant, while also being adapted for temporary event use.

---

## Music and Event Core

A key defining element of the target is **live music performance**.

- A stage is placed within the castle setting
- Music is actively being played
- The crowd gathers to listen, watch, and participate
- The stone environment shapes how the sound and atmosphere feel

The purpose of the gathering is clearly:

- attending the festival
- listening to live music
- socializing within the event environment

This is not casual sightseeing. It is an active cultural event centered on sound and performance.

---

## Activity and Movement

The scene contains continuous, layered activity.

### Large-scale movement
- People entering and leaving through the gate
- Flow between the town and the castle
- Movement along paths and slopes

### Small-scale movement
- Conversations in small groups
- Sitting on walls and edges
- Standing and observing the surroundings
- Drinking and relaxing
- Taking photos and documenting the moment

The movement pattern is:

**flow → pause → interaction → movement again**

The energy is social, steady, and distributed across the area rather than concentrated in one place.

---

## Clothing and Visual Identity

The clothing strongly reflects an **alternative and gothic subculture**.

- Black is the dominant color
- Long coats, dresses, and layered outfits
- Heavy boots and platform footwear
- Fishnet tights and patterned leggings
- Hooded garments and stylized robes
- Accessories such as sunglasses, hats, and symbolic details
- Visible makeup and expressive styling

Some people wear more casual clothing, but the overall visual identity remains cohesive and intentional.

The clothing style is an important part of the event atmosphere itself.

---

## Human Behavior and Posture

- Relaxed and social body language
- Leaning on walls or standing in place
- Sitting on stone edges
- Facing one another during conversation
- Turning toward the direction of the stage and music
- Holding drinks or devices

The crowd behaves like a temporary community rather than a passing group of visitors.

---

## Objects and Environmental Details

Visible objects include:

- Bottles and cups
- Cameras and photography equipment
- Bags and backpacks
- Event signage, maps, and boards
- Festival-related props and decorative items

These details reinforce the feeling of a long-duration outdoor event.

---

## Atmosphere

- Open-air daylight with strong shadows
- A blend of historical gravity and modern subculture
- Social, expressive, and relaxed
- Structured by the shape of the castle and the movement of the crowd

---

## Summary

The target is a **castle-based music festival** with the following key components:

- A hill, castle, and town system
- A medieval stone structure adapted for events
- A live music stage located within the castle
- A crowd gathered primarily to listen to performances
- A strong gothic and alternative visual identity
- Continuous social interaction and movement

The core idea of the target is the fusion of:

**history + music + people**

creating a living festival environment inside a castle.
