# Example Signal Library

## Pattern: Isolated technical vertical structure
- Category: Structure / human-made
- Field signal:
  - high, narrow, vertical mass
  - hard, cold, stable
  - isolated rather than embedded
  - upper point, spike, mast, or light-like anomaly
- What it can be confused with:
  - mountain peak
  - tower inside a larger city/port system
  - symbolic monument
- Best discriminating test:
  - check surface: rough natural irregularity vs built geometry
  - check isolation: part of a larger mass vs single technical form
  - check for point-like technical energy or light at the upper section
- Confirmed in session:
  - Armen Lighthouse
- Notes:
  - In this case the vertical structure was correct, but the surrounding land system was overbuilt in interpretation.

## Pattern: Water movement mistaken for organized transport
- Category: Motion / environment
- Field signal:
  - repeating movement
  - directionality
  - rhythm
  - sense of route or passage
- What it can be confused with:
  - heavy land vehicles
  - corridor traffic
  - rail or road transport
- Best discriminating test:
  - does the movement pulse like waves?
  - is the medium soft, cold, fluid, and non-rigid?
  - do separate moving masses feel floating rather than rolling?
- Confirmed in session:
  - Armen Lighthouse
- Notes:
  - Ship movement plus wave rhythm was translated too early into land traffic.

## Pattern: Scale distortion from field intensity
- Category: Scale / interpretation filter
- Field signal:
  - something feels larger than its physical size
  - emotional or energetic importance exceeds actual dimensions
  - viewer tends to monumentalize the form
- What it can be confused with:
  - mountain
  - giant monument
  - complex infrastructure
- Best discriminating test:
  - compare against human scale
  - imagine the form at hand-size and ask whether it becomes functional
  - ask whether the field is large, or the object is large
- Confirmed in session:
  - recurring Orion training note
- Notes:
  - Strong field does not always mean huge object.

## Pattern: Crowd-wave vs water-wave
- Category: Motion / biology vs environment
- Field signal:
  - large-scale wave-like motion
  - repeating rhythm
  - broad field undulation
- What it can be confused with:
  - sea, surf, floodwater
  - crowd, stadium, mass human reaction
- Best discriminating test:
  - water = uniform, fluid, impersonal
  - crowd = many points, emotional spikes, variable rhythm
  - listen for monotone noise vs layered human sound
- Confirmed in session:
  - Orion training note
- Notes:
  - Do not label wave motion as water before checking individuality, atmosphere, and sound character.
